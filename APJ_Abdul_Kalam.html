A small ode to a great soul

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Fondly called the “Missile Man of India,” Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. His life is a beacon of humility, dedication, and relentless curiosity.

Visionary Leader

He inspired millions with his dream of transforming India into a developed nation.

Lifelong Learner

From newspaper boy to top scientist, he proved learning never stops.

Humility & Kindness

Despite accolades, Dr. Kalam remained humble and approachable to all.

Passion for Youth

He loved interacting with students, urging them to dream big and serve the nation.
